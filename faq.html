FAQ Section

Question 1: What is the Battle Pass?

The Battle Pass rewards players with in-game items by playing the game and completing challenges. The more you play, the more rewards you unlock. Level up even faster by knocking out those daily challenges.

Question 2: How do they handle teamers in solo play?

The developers have built a system to automatically ban teamers in solo play.

Question 3: Do I need a Console to play Battle Royale?

No. You do not need Console in order to play Battle Royale.

Question 4: Will Bluetooth headsets be supported on Android devices?

Yes. Battle Royale Games currently support the use of Bluetooth headsets for audio usage but not for voice chat.

Question 5: Will Android players be forced to play with other platforms?

No. By default, Android and iOS players are matched against other mobile players only. However, if a mobile player joins a crossplay party, they'll be placed in crossplay matchmaking queues. Note: Any players using controllers will be automatically placed in crossplay lobbies with other players who are using gamepads.
